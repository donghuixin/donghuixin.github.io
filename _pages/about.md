I collaborate closely with [Prof. Jie Xiong](https://people.cs.umass.edu/~jxiong/) and [Prof. Lili Qiu](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/~lili/) at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) on GPS sensing.
